About Us

Manatec Electronics Pvt. Ltd is an ISO certified company engaged in manufacture, supply and export of automobile service station equipments viz., computerized wheel aligners, wheel balancers, tyre changers, two post lifts, pollution checking equipments, automatic tyre inflators etc., Manatec is the first company in Asia to manufacture computerised wheel aligners and wheel balancers. Manatec has an in-house R&D, recognised by Govt of India. All the products manufactured are designed and developed by the in-house R&D. The R&D team comprises of high qualified professionals.

  • The company has widespread sales and service network in India having branch offices in Chennai, Mumbai, Kolkata, New Delhi, Guwahati and Bangalore.
  • Manatec's products are highly appreciated in overseas markets for its sustained quality. Products are exported to Brazil, Colombia, Ghana, Nigeria, Zambia, Kenya, Egypt, Oman, U.A.E, Bahrain, Saudi Arabia, Mauritius, SriLanka, Bangladesh, Indonesia, NewZealand etc.,
  • The company has won many awards for excellence in business. Recently it received the Star Performer Regional Award for Export Excellence consecutively for the years 2006-07 & 2007-08 from EEPC INDIA.
  • The products are approved by all the vehicle & tyre manufacturers in India viz., Maruti, Tata motors, Hyundai, Ford, Mahindra, Skoda, Eicher, Hindustan Motors, Nissan , Appollo, JK Tyres, Ceat, Bridgestone, Goodyear, Reliance, Essar, HPCL, BPCL, IOCL, Elf gas etc.,

Services

Live Tracking

Track and monitor all your fleet assets effortlessly using a live map. Powerful auto-position feature updates your fleet as they move real-time with heading-changes.

Tyre Pressure Monitoring System

Tyre Pressure Monitoring System keeps in check the pressure and temperature levels of each and every tyre in your fleet.

Real-Time Alerts

Stay on top of your fleet without constant monitoring. Be alerted round the clock through email or text messages for any kind of deviations in fleet activity.

Geo-Fencing

Create virtual perimeters anywhere on the map and get alerted for any activity associated with that landmark.

Vehicle Speed Monitoring

Effectively monitor how fast your fleet vehicles are driven. Enhance safety, reduce fuel consumption by spotting over-speeding drivers.

Fleet Data Management

Manage and analyze entire fleet data with a single web portal.
